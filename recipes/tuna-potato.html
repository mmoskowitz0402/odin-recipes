A tuna potato is member only food requiring 68 Cooking to make and grants a total of 309.5 Cooking experience once all preparation steps are complete. They are considered one of the best foods as they restore 22 hitpoints, the highest of single-bite foods, along with dark crab and manta ray â€” as well as anglerfish with 93+ Hitpoints. At 0.5kg each, tuna potatoes weigh slightly more than manta rays (0.4 kg) and dark crabs (0.3 kg).

Ingredients:

  • A potato
  • Raw sweetcorn
  • Bucket of milk OR pot of cream
  • A bowl
  • A knife

Procedure:

  1. Cook a raw potato on a range to make a baked potato
  2. Churn a bucket of milk or pot of cream into a pat of butter
  3. Use the butter on the baked potato to make a potato with butter
  4. Cook raw tuna on a range to make tuna
  5. Cook sweetcorn on a range to make cooked sweetcorn
  6. Use the tuna on a bowl, with a knife, to make chopped tuna
  7. Use the sweetcorn on the chopped tuna to make tuna and corn
  8. Use the tuna and corn on the potato with butter to make a tuna potato
